O.P Jindal School, Angul is a Co-educational, English Medium Day school with a vision of over 3000 students. Following the curriculum of the Central Board of Secondary Education,Delhi, The school runs by Jindal Welfare Society. It is situated amidst lush green congenial surroundings away from the pollution of city life. The school building incorporates the latest in layout and design. The priority at O.P. Jindal School, Angul is academic excellence and value based education in tune with our rich culture and traditions. A spiritual undercurrent remains enshrined in all educational processes and co-curricular activities that cater to the academic and creative demands of students hailing from the dive.
